Q. So how much is it for my kid to perform for y'all.

A. Band dues, which expenses change yearly, help pay for our band's equipment, buses, food, and other necessities. It is currently only $250 to join the Adamsville band


Q. Are there ways to support the band?

A. We have many fundraisers around the year, so there will almost certainly be ways to help us. We also take sponsorships, so if you are interested contact Mr. Young or another staff member of the Adamsville Band
